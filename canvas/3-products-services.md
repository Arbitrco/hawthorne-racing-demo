# Block 3 | Products/Services

What Hawthorne Racing delivers, and where AI touches each offering.

## Core value delivered

1. **On-track performance.** Two race entries per Grand Prix; constructors' points are
   the product. AI impact: strategy simulation synthesis, decision support, debrief
   intelligence.
2. **Sponsor value.** Brand exposure, hospitality, B2B introductions, content rights.
   AI impact: reporting automation, activation ideation, renewal risk detection.
3. **Fan engagement.** Content, community, and merchandise that fund the racing.
   AI impact: content drafting within brand voice, audience insight summaries.

## Key features AI will impact first

| Offering | AI enhancement | Status |
|----------|----------------|--------|
| Race strategy meetings | Decision extraction, bias detection, longitudinal tracking | Live |
| Post-race debriefs | Automated decision ledger with owner and rationale capture | Live |
| Sponsor monthly reports | Data-to-narrative generation in sponsor-specific templates | Pilot |
| Partnership proposals | Draft generation from prospect research | Pilot |
| Factory SOPs | Documentation drafting and maintenance | Backlog |

## What AI does not touch

Car design IP, aerodynamic data interpretation, and anything covered by FIA
technical regulations remains human-only. See Block 8 (Rules).
