# Block 2 | Company

## Identity

Hawthorne Racing is an independent Formula 1 constructor. Midfield budget,
front-running ambition. The team competes against organisations with two to three
times its headcount and treats operational discipline as its competitive equaliser.

## Mission

Outscore bigger budgets through faster, better-quality decisions.

## Values

- **Evidence before instinct.** Instinct starts the conversation; data finishes it.
- **Own the call.** Every significant decision has a named owner and a recorded rationale.
- **Speed with a paper trail.** Fast decisions still get documented. The debrief is sacred.
- **No silos.** Trackside, factory, and commercial share one source of truth.

## Business objectives this AI initiative supports

1. Reduce strategy decision latency during race weekends without sacrificing rigour.
2. Convert post-race debriefs into a compounding decision intelligence asset.
3. Cut sponsor reporting effort by 60 percent while improving renewal rates.
4. Protect institutional knowledge against staff poaching by rival teams.

## Alignment with overall strategy

The team cannot outspend rivals, so it invests in decision quality per dollar. AI is
positioned as a force multiplier for the existing 380-person workforce, not a
replacement for expertise.
