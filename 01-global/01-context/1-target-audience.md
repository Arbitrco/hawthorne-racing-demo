# Block 1 | Target Audience

Who benefits from AI at Hawthorne Racing, and what they need from it.

## Primary audiences

### Race leadership (Team Principal, Sporting Director)
- Need: fast, defensible decisions under time pressure. Pit calls, penalty responses,
  driver management.
- Pain: decisions made in seconds get relitigated for weeks. No record of why a call
  was made or who owned it.
- AI interaction: decision briefs, meeting analysis, pre-mortems before high-stakes calls.

### Engineering leadership (Technical Director, Head of Strategy)
- Need: synthesis across simulation output, tyre data, and competitor intelligence.
- Pain: data volume exceeds human review capacity between sessions.
- AI interaction: race weekend briefing packs, anomaly summaries, strategy option trees.

### Commercial team (Commercial Director, Partnerships Managers)
- Need: sponsor reporting, renewal narratives, activation ideas at pace.
- Pain: every sponsor wants bespoke reporting; the team rebuilds the same deck monthly.
- AI interaction: automated sponsor reports, proposal drafting, market scans.

## Secondary audiences

- Factory operations staff: process documentation, onboarding, compliance checklists.
- Drivers and performance staff: debrief summaries, media prep briefs.

## What all audiences share

Time poverty, high accountability, and low tolerance for generic output. Every AI
deliverable must be specific to Hawthorne data and decision-ready, not a starting point
for more work.
