every workspace: race-weekend cadence and decision deadlines for Race Operations,
fan-hype windows for Commercial. Public information only, no strategy content.
Dates are the on-track weekend (Fri to Sun).

Source: formula1.com/en/racing/2026 and motorsport.com/f1/schedule/2026,
retrieved 2026-07-20. 24 rounds, 21 countries. If a date or venue is corrected by
the FIA, update this file and note the change.

## Operational notes

- **Home race:** Round 11, British GP (Silverstone). Team base race.
- **Sprint weekends (6):** Rounds 2, 6, 7, 11, 14, 18 (China, Miami, Canada, Great
  Britain, Netherlands, Singapore). Compressed schedule, one practice session,
  parc ferme locks after SQ. Plan briefs accordingly.
- **Season boundaries:** opens Round 1 Australia (2026-03-06), closes Round 24 Abu
  Dhabi (2026-12-06).
- **Ramadan note:** Bahrain and Saudi Arabia run in April in 2026, later than usual.
- **Two Spanish rounds:** Round 9 Barcelona-Catalunya (June) and Round 16 Spanish GP
  at Madrid (September) are distinct events at distinct venues.
