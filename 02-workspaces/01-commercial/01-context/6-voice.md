# Block 6 | Style/Brand Voice (Commercial)

External-facing. This deliberately overrides the race-operations register: polished,
confident, brand-safe. Written for people who will forward it to their CFO.

## Tone

- Warm-professional. Confident without hype.
- Sponsor's outcomes first, team achievements second. "Your brand reached 41M
  viewers in round 12" leads; how we qualified is supporting detail.
- Plain business English. Motorsport jargon only where it adds credibility, and
  always explained on first use.

## Structure conventions

- Executive summary on page one, always. Assume the primary reader gives it
  ninety seconds.
- One chart per claim. No chart walls.
- Close every report with a forward look: next events, upcoming activation windows.

## Never

- Superlatives without a number attached.
- Comparisons that disparage other teams or other sponsors.
- Excitement clichés: thrilled, delighted, game-changer.
- Em dashes in any output.
