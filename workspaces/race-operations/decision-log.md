# Race Operations Decision Log

Shared, tracked-in-repo record of decisions made in this workspace — the
human-readable companion to `data/decision-ledger.json` (Block 7). Every
significant decision gets a named owner and a recorded rationale, per
`canvas/2-company.md`: "the debrief is sacred."

Populated primarily via the `meeting-decision-analysis.md` request
(Block 9), which also appends the structured entry to
`data/decision-ledger.json`. Add manually for decisions made outside a
logged meeting.

| # | Decision | Owner | Method | Evidence / Rationale | Reversibility | Status | Date |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|

Method taxonomy (from Block 7): consensus, consultative, delegated,
directive, escalated.
